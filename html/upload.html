Generate A Random Empires Mod

Here you can generate and download a mod pack with a random selection of empires from the pool.

Unzip this in your mod folder, and you should see a file random-empires.mod and a folder random-empires. When you next launch Stellaris, you should be able to enable the "Random Empires Mod" in the mods section of the launcher.

Mod Folder Location

The location of your mod depends on your operating system and how you are running Stellaris. The following are correct for games launched from Steam.

  • %USERPROFILE%\Documents\Paradox Interactive\Stellaris\mod\ [Windows]
  • ~/Documents/Paradox Interactive/Stellaris/mod/ [Mac]
  • ~/.local/share/Paradox Interactive/Stellaris/mod/
